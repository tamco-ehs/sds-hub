    Safety & AI disclaimer

    This platform provides quick access to SDS and chemical safety information. Some data may be extracted, summarised or assisted by automated tools, including AI. Such information is provided for reference only and must not be treated as the official SDS. The approved manufacturer or supplier SDS remains the source of truth.

    AI-extracted data may be incorrect, incomplete, outdated or misread. Users must verify safety-critical information against the original SDS before using any chemical, including hazards, PPE, handling, storage, first aid, spill response, firefighting and disposal requirements.

    Where information is unclear, missing, conflicting or safety-critical, users must refer to the original SDS and consult EHS before proceeding.
